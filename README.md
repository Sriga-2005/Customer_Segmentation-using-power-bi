
# Customer Segmentation Dashboard using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on **Customer Segmentation Analysis** using **Power BI**.
The dashboard helps businesses understand customer purchasing behavior, payment preferences, regional performance, customer types, and spending patterns.

By analyzing customer data visually, businesses can make smarter marketing decisions, improve customer engagement, and identify high-value customer groups.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer purchasing behavior
* Segment customers based on spending and order patterns
* Identify preferred payment methods
* Compare regional and country-wise performance
* Understand customer types such as:

  * New Customers
  * Returning Customers
  * VIP Customers
  * Loyal Customers
  * At-Risk Customers
* Visualize business insights using interactive dashboards

---

# 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Data Visualization
* DAX Measures
* Data Cleaning & Transformation
* Interactive Filters & Slicers

# 📊 Dashboard Features

## 1️⃣ KPI Cards

The dashboard displays important business metrics:

* Total Amount Spent
* Total Purchases
* Total Orders
* Most Preferred Payment Method

### Example Insights

* Total Spent: **264K**
* Total Purchases: **1338**
* Total Orders: **1529**
* Most Preferred Payment Method: **Credit Card**

---

## 2️⃣ Regional Purchase Analysis

Treemap visualization showing:

* Total purchases by region
* Gender-wise distribution across regions

### Regions Included

* North
* South
* East
* West

### Insights

* South region shows higher purchase activity
* Female customers contribute significantly in some regions

---

## 3️⃣ Payment Method Analysis

Comparison of customer types based on payment methods:

* PayPal
* Credit Card
* Debit Card

### Customer Categories

* New Customers
* Returning Customers
* VIP Customers

### Insights

* Returning customers use PayPal more frequently
* VIP customers prefer Debit/Credit cards

---

## 4️⃣ Country-wise Customer Analysis

Analysis based on countries:

* USA
* India
* Canada
* UK
* Australia
* Germany
* UAE

### Metrics Included

* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Customer Category Distribution

---

## 5️⃣ Spending Pattern by Age

Visualization of:

* Total spending by age group
* Country-wise spending comparison

### Insights

* Customers aged between 25–40 contribute the highest spending
* India and USA show strong customer activity

---

## 6️⃣ Referral Source Analysis

Customer spending analyzed based on referral sources:

* Facebook
* Google
* Instagram
* Friend Referrals

### Insights

* Facebook and Instagram contribute more customer spending

## 7️⃣ Membership Segmentation
